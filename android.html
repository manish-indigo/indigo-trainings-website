Automation with Android
Modern automation systems provide the option to actually integrate more than just an HMI to our industrial automation systems. This course is focused at understanding what could possibly be the future of industrial automation where open source hardware and software solutions will make industrial automation availabe to everyone. The course focuses on understanding application development on smart phones that can handle factory equipment and can even monitor the equipments if neede.
